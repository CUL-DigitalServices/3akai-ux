What's changing?

HEFCE has a new policy for Open Access in the next Research Excellence Framework (REF).

The policy requires you to upload your accepted, peer-reviewed articles and conference proceedings to an institutional repository.

We will continue to advise on other funders’ policies and to pay eligible Open Access costs for RCUK and Wellcome Trust-funded papers.

Upload your accepted manuscripts and we’ll take care of the rest.

What type of manuscripts are affected?

All accepted journal articles and conference proceedings (with an ISSN) should be uploaded, to ensure they are eligible for the next REF.

This policy does not apply to monographs, book chapters or other long-form publications, working papers, creative or practice-based research outputs or data.

When do I need to do something?

As soon as your peer-reviewed manuscript has been accepted, and before signing a Copyright Transfer Agreement or Open Access form, upload it.

HEFCE policy requires manuscripts to be uploaded no later than three months after acceptance. This policy comes into force on 1 April 2016. Yet at the University we want to ensure this shift is managed sooner rather than later, so that no research is omitted.

Uploading at acceptance lets us check your options and advise you how to fill in copyright or Open Access forms to comply with other funders’ requirements, and – in eligible cases – to commit funds to cover Open Access fees.

Research Excellence Framework (REF)

By uploading your peer-reviewed manuscript as soon as it is accepted by a publisher, we’ll make sure you comply with HEFCE’s Open Access policy for the next REF.

Publisher embargoes are respected (the period when you can only access an article with a paid access). Manuscripts must be free to read and download after the following periods:

  • 12 months for STEM subjects
  • 24 months for Humanities subjects

How does this apply to other funders?

Only the HEFCE policy is new. Other funders’ Open Access policies have not changed. If you upload a manuscript we’ll make sure the article complies with all the funder policies that apply.

All papers funded by RCUK or Wellcome must be made Open Access, to comply with their requirements. We will continue to assist with this.

Upload your manuscript and we’ll guide you through how to make it compliant.
